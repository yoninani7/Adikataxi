About Adika Taxi

Two decades of excellence in reliable transportation services

Our Story

Founded in 2003, Adika Taxi began as a small family-owned business with just three vehicles. Our founder, Eskinder Taye, envisioned a transportation service that would prioritize safety, reliability, and exceptional customer service above all else.

Over the past two decades, we've grown from a local taxi service to one of the region's most trusted transportation providers. Today, we operate a diverse fleet of over 200 vehicles and employ more than 300 dedicated professionals who share our commitment to excellence.
